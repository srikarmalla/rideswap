Our Story

RideSwap was born in 2025 when our founders, a group of travel enthusiasts and tech innovators, recognized a significant gap in the vehicle rental market. They envisioned a platform that would combine convenience, affordability, and reliability for all types of travelers. What began as a small startup with just a handful of vehicles in one city has now grown into a nationwide network with thousands of premium vehicles across multiple categories.
